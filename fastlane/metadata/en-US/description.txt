Winch Pilot is a real-time voice assistant for glider pilots during winch launches. It monitors your speed, altitude, and attitude using your device's sensors and provides configurable voice callouts at critical moments.

KEY FEATURES

- Real-time speed monitoring with configurable min/max thresholds and voice alerts
- QFE altitude display using barometer, with customizable altitude callouts
- Artificial horizon showing pitch and roll attitude
- Remaining runway distance indicator
- Wing drop detection with automatic voice warning
- Heading indicator (landscape mode)
- Altitude history chart for post-launch review

SMART SENSOR FUSION

Winch Pilot combines GPS speed with accelerometer data through a Kalman filter for smooth, responsive speed readings. Barometer-based altitude (QFE) provides accurate height above field level, updated at high frequency.

VOICE CALLOUTS

Configure voice alerts for:
- Minimum safe speed reached / lost
- Overspeed warning
- Altitude milestones (fully customizable)
- Wing drop detection
- Maximum altitude at launch completion

Adjust speech rate and customize every callout message.

FLIGHT LOGGING

Record sensor data from each launch for later review and replay. Logs include accelerometer, GPS, barometer, and attitude data at up to 50Hz.

UNITS & SETTINGS

Choose between km/h, mph, or knots for speed, and meters or feet for altitude. Calibrate pitch and roll zero offsets for your specific mount.

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

This app is a supplementary aid only. The pilot in command remains solely responsible for all decisions during flight. Never rely on this app as a primary source of information.
